About Brain Tumors

A brain tumor is an abnormal growth of cells within the brain or skull. These tumors may be benign or malignant, and early detection is essential.

Signs & Symptoms

Symptoms vary depending on the tumor location and size. Common signs include headaches, vomiting, blurred vision, seizures, and difficulty speaking or balancing.

How the System Works

Our system uses deep learning models to analyze MRI images and detect tumors with high accuracy. The model classifies tumor types and provides prediction confidence levels.
